  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs

Notes

For softer cookies, try using half brown sugar and half white sugar. This provides a perfect balance of crisp edges with a soft middle.

Chill your dough before baking to enhance the flavor and prevent the cookies from spreading too much.

Experiment with different types of chocolate for a unique taste. Mixing dark, milk, and white chocolate can create a delightful variety of flavors.
